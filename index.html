A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile man of India

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist who joined India's defence department aftergraduating from the Madras Institute of Technology. He was a central figure in thedevelopment of the country's nuclear capabilities and was hailed as a national hero after aseries of successful tests in 1998. Kalam served as India's president for one term from 2002 to 2007, and died of a heart attack on July 27, 2015.
